Gee's 13 Learning Principles

Learning is built step by step, like constructing a building!
From bottom to top: Build the foundation → Practice on it → Form deep understanding at the top
1
Foundation
The base: Establish learner agency, identity, and tool capability
1
Co-Design
Active producers, not passive consumers
2
Customize
Learn in your preferred way
3
Identity
Identify with a valued role
4
Manipulation & Distributed Knowledge
Extend capability through smart tools
↑ With foundation established, practice can begin
2
Practice
Learning by doing: Growth through well-designed practice and challenges
5
Well-Ordered Problems
Problems sequenced from simple to complex
6
Pleasantly Frustrating
Challenging but achievable
7
Cycles of Expertise
Practice → Mastery → New challenge → Re-learn
8
Information "On Demand" & "Just in Time"
Information when you need it
9
Fish Tanks
Simplify first, highlight key variables
10
Sandboxes
Safe space for experimentation
11
Skills as Strategies
Skills as means to achieve goals
↑ Accumulated through practice, finally forming deep understanding
3
Understanding
Ultimate goal: Form systematic deep understanding through embodied experience
12
System Thinking
See relationships and the whole system
13
Meaning as Action Image
Understand meaning through experience and action
